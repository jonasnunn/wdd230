Allen Chamber of Commerece

Providing a place for local bussinsses to network and for consumers to stay informed about their community.

allenchamberofcommerce.com

Target Audience: The target audience is business owners who operate companies in Allen and consumers who want to learn about what the businesses in the community are.

Personas

Persona: CEO Benny's Bagels

Name: Benny Bagette

Demographics: 55 years old, married, has two kids

Purpose: He wants to share what his business has to offer to the community and get to know other local business owners.

Persona: Consumer

Name: Caren

Demographics: 48 years old, single, no children

Purpose: She wants to learn more about what specials or deals local businesses might be having.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: A middle aged business man wants to promote a community event his business will be putting on.

Scenario 2: A job seeking college graduate wants to see what networking events his communnity might be having.

Scenario 3: A prospective enutrapenur wants to learn about the laws and regulations relateing to businesses.

Scenario 4: A resident wants to see what special offers and events are occuring.

Scenario 5: Someone new in the city wants to learn about the local bussiness.
